About the Role

Role Overview:

As the Tech & Development Co-Founder, you will lead the technical development of the SkilWise platform from the ground up. You’ll work closely with the founder to shape the product vision and execute the technical roadmap. Your role will involve designing and developing the platform, implementing scalable solutions, and driving innovation in the recruitment tech space.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Lead the design, development, and scaling of the SkilWise platform
  • Build and maintain the platform’s backend, frontend, and infrastructure
  • Collaborate with the founder and marketing teams to align technical features with business goals
  • Drive technical innovation to ensure the platform remains cutting-edge in the recruitment industry
  • Oversee technical decisions, including architecture, frameworks, and toolsets
  • Maintain a high level of quality assurance and testing across the platform
  • Guide future technical hires and grow the tech team
  • Ensure the platform is secure, reliable, and scalable as we grow

Requirements

  • Proven Experience in platform development, coding, and AI solutions
  • Strong proficiency in relevant programming languages (e.g., Python, JavaScript, etc.)
  • Experience in building and scaling tech products from scratch
  • Entrepreneurial mindset with a drive to take initiative and make strategic decisions
  • Excellent problem-solving and critical-thinking skills
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced, dynamic startup environment
  • Strong communication and collaboration skills, with the ability to work well in a remote team
  • Leadership experience and the ability to guide technical teams as the company grows
  • Passion for innovating and disrupting the hiring process
  • Willingness to take on an equity-based role in a growing startup

About the Company

At SkilWise, we’re on a mission to transform the hiring process. We believe that skills should speak louder than experience, and we’re building a platform that empowers both job seekers and recruiters to connect based on real, verifiable abilities. We’re a startup in its early stages, with a passionate founder dedicated to revolutionizing the way people are hired.

We’re looking for a tech-savvy co-founder who shares our vision and is ready to play a pivotal role in bringing this idea to life. As we build the future of hiring, we want you to be part of something impactful, innovative, and exciting. This is an opportunity to work closely with a driven team and take ownership of the technical side of the business while shaping the direction of SkilWise. If you’re ready to make a lasting impact, we want to hear from you.
